Who We Are

The Youth Health Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to addressing the health challenges faced by young people, particularly in preventing HIV, improving mental health, and providing access to healthcare. We aim to empower the youth with knowledge and tools to lead healthier lives.

Our Mission

To provide holistic health education and prevention programs, focusing on youth development, HIV prevention, mental health awareness, and access to healthcare.

Our Vision

To see a generation of youth who are empowered to make informed health decisions, leading to a future with lower HIV infection rates and better health outcomes for all.

Our Programs

HIV Prevention

We provide comprehensive education on HIV prevention strategies, distribute resources such as condoms and PrEP, and raise awareness through campaigns.

Mental Health Support

We offer mental health resources, counseling services, and workshops to help young people cope with stress, anxiety, and other mental health challenges.

Community Outreach

Our outreach teams visit hospitals and schools to conduct health workshops, provide screenings, and offer support for vulnerable youth populations.

Health Education

We organize seminars and webinars to educate young people on general health topics, including nutrition, sexual health, and the importance of regular check-ups.
